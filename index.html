Services

Custom PC Building

We don't just sell parts; we build your dream machine. Tell us your needs (gaming, video editing, general use), your budget, and we'll hand-pick the perfect components. You get a high-performance PC tailored to you, built by experts, and tested before delivery. We'll even help you choose the right aesthetics!

Computer Repair

Is your computer running slow, crashing, or showing strange errors? Our technicians diagnose the problem, explain it clearly, and offer cost-effective solutions. We handle everything from virus removal to hardware repairs, getting you back online quickly.

Upgrading Existing Systems

Don't replace your computer upgrade it! We can boost your system's performance by adding faster RAM, a larger SSD, or a more powerful graphics card. We'll assess your current setup and recommend the best upgrades to meet your budget and needs.

Computer Part Sales

We stock a wide range of high-quality components from leading brands. Whether you're building a new PC or need a replacement part, we have what you need. Our knowledgeable staff can help you choose the right parts for your project, ensuring compatibility and optimal performance.

Software Installation & Support

We'll get your software up and running smoothly. From installing a fresh operating system to setting up your favorite applications, we'll handle it all. We also provide basic troubleshooting for software-related issues.

Tech Support

Need help with a specific problem? Our tech support team is here to assist you. We offer phone, email, and in-person support to answer your questions and resolve your technical issues. We aim to provide clear, helpful solutions to keep your technology running smoothly.
